What is it?

India's First Digital Census

🏠

Self-Enumeration (SE)

For the first time, citizens can submit their own household data online — before an enumerator visits. You fill the form, get an SE ID, and share it with the official.

📱

Fully Digital Process

Census 2027 is conducted digitally. Enumerators use mobile apps, and citizens can self-enumerate through the official web portal at se.census.gov.in.

🌐

16 Indian Languages

The self-enumeration portal is available in 16 languages including Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, and more.

Two Phases

How Census 2027 Works

Phase I

House Listing & Housing Census (HLO)

Captures information about your home's construction, available amenities, and household assets. Includes a 15-day Self-Enumeration window before the 30-day house listing period.

📅 April – September 2026 (varies by state)
Phase II

Population Enumeration

Captures individual-level data for every person in the household. Includes caste enumeration — a first since 1931. The reference date is 00:00 hrs on 1st March 2027.

📅 February 2027
Step-by-Step Guide

How to Fill the Online Census Form

1

Visit the Official Self-Enumeration Portal

Open your browser and go to se.census.gov.in — the official Government of India census self-enumeration portal. Ensure you are using the official URL and not a third-party site.

Use Chrome or Firefox for best compatibility. The portal works on mobile and desktop.
2

Select Your Preferred Language

The portal is available in 16 Indian languages. On the home page, select your preferred language from the dropdown. Options include Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Kannada, Marathi, Odia, Malayalam, and more.

3

Register Using Your Mobile Number

The head of the household must register. Enter your 10-digit mobile number on the login page. You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) via SMS. Enter the OTP to verify your account.

Keep your phone handy — the OTP expires in a few minutes. No Aadhaar or ID document is required to register.
4

Enter Your Address & Location Details

Provide your complete address — state, district, tehsil/block, village/ward, and house number. Make sure these match your physical address as enumerators will verify this during the house visit.

Double-check your house number carefully. Errors here can cause verification issues when the enumerator arrives.
5

Fill Housing & Amenities Details

Answer questions about your dwelling — its condition (good / liveable / dilapidated), construction material used for floors, walls, and roof (e.g., pucca/kachcha), number of rooms, and ownership type (owned/rented).

6

Provide Amenities & Asset Information

Fill in details about access to: drinking water source, toilet type, cooking fuel, lighting source, drainage, bathing facility, and household assets such as TV, radio, mobile phone, internet, laptop, bicycle, motorbike, car, and more.

All answers are self-reported and based on your actual household situation — be honest and accurate.
7

Enter Household Member Details

Provide the number of members living in the household. In Phase I (HLO), you do not need to fill individual member details — that comes in Phase II (Population Enumeration) in February 2027.

8

Review & Submit the Form

Carefully review all the information you have filled in. Once you are satisfied that all details are correct, click the Submit button. You can preview each section before final submission.

You cannot edit the form after final submission, so review everything carefully before clicking Submit.
9

Receive & Save Your SE ID

After successful submission, you will receive a unique Self-Enumeration ID (SE ID). Note this down or take a screenshot. This ID will be sent to your registered mobile number as well.

10

Share SE ID with the Enumerator

When the government enumerator visits your home during the 30-day house listing period, share your SE ID with them. They will verify your self-submitted data and mark your household as complete in their mobile app.

The enumerator may still ask a few additional questions. Cooperate and provide accurate information.

📋 What You Need

  • Active mobile phone number
  • Your home address (house no., village/ward)
  • Details about your home's construction
  • Knowledge of household amenities
  • List of household assets
  • Number of household members

✅ Key Points to Remember

  • No Aadhaar or ID card required
  • Portal available in 16 languages
  • Process takes about 10–15 minutes
  • Head of household must register
  • SE window is 15 days before house listing
  • SE ID is proof of successful submission

📅 State-wise SE Schedule (Phase I)

  • From April 1: Goa, Sikkim, Mizoram, Odisha, Karnataka, A&N Islands, NDMC, Delhi Cantonment
  • From April 16: Andhra Pradesh, Haryana (approx.)
  • From May 1: Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Jharkhand, Delhi (MCD areas) and many more states

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I fill it on mobile? Yes — portal is mobile-friendly
  • What if I miss the SE window? An enumerator will visit your home
  • Is my data secure? Yes — government data security measures apply
  • Can I edit after submitting? No — review carefully before submitting
  • What language can I use? Any of the 16 available Indian languages
